    In kitchen training & demo

    We have a team of food chefs and beverage chefs to understand unique requirement of every customer. Food chefs/Beverage chefs visit customer kitchen/bar facility to understand fitment of solution in customer’s setup. Products are demonstrated at customer facility to be sure of fitment and integration.Once product solutions are finalized, food chef/beverage chef ensure training of the team for smooth operations ensuring perfect service for diners

    Standardise recipe

    Major problem in any food business is consistency of taste of any dish. Diners come back to you for the experience they loved last time they visited your outlet. But due to changes in ingredient quality, variation in the recipe with different chefs, taste varies. This can lose your repeat customer.Our solutions ensure consistency of ingredient, there is a science behind every taste – once you fix the dosage of application, the taste of your dish will remain same no matter who makes it and in which season
